Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 23, 2025

STS Automator ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application and its integration with the Pinterest platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you use STS Automator with your Pinterest account, we may access certain information based on the permissions you grant:

  • Profile Information: Your Pinterest username, profile image, and basic account details.
  • Content Data: Information about your Boards, Pins, and other content you create or manage on Pinterest.
  • Usage Data: Data about how you interact with our automation tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the purpose of providing and improving the STS Automator service:

  • To authenticate your identity via Pinterest.
  • To perform the automation tasks you have configured (e.g., creating pins, managing boards).
  • To ensure the technical stability and security of our application.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With Pinterest: As part of the API integration, certain data is exchanged with Pinterest to execute your commands.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide the services you have requested and for legitimate business purposes.

5. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
